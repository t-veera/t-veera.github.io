Hasmart  Irrigation



What is it?

Hasmart Irrigation provides automatic water distribution across the field in crop cultivation. It is highly reliable, robust and durable. It is easy to install in the field and operates automatically with remote monitoring and control through smartphone. This is ideally suited for crop under drip cultivation, horticulture, floriculture and forestry and can be easily used by farmers.

Features:

  • Irrigation Management: Automatic distribution of water is managed by the system without any human intervention.

  • Maintenance Free: System is completely maintenance free and user-friendly.

  • Monitor Status: Status of the system on field can be monitored from anywhere in the world using the android application provided.

  • Wireless and Battery Operated: System is completely wireless and operates on low power DC supply, and is independent of high power line supply making it safe to use on the field.

  • Customizable Watering Time and Soil Wetness Conditions: Moisture levels in the soil can be set according to the crop's condition. Also, the amount of time to water the crops can be. These settings can be changed using the android app provided with the system.

  • Safety and Protection:

    • Voltage and Current Protection for Pump: Auto pump shut off when the pump draws more current and operates in voltages beyond ratings.

    • Battery Short-Circuit Protection: Auto battery shut off when system draws current more than rated, protecting the battery and the system.

    • Overload Protection for Valve: Drive motor for valve is protected against mechanical overload when the valve is jammed.

    • System Notifications: Receive alert notifications on your mobile device for current system status.

    • Remote Emergency Shutdown: In case of emergency, shutdown the system completely from a remote location via mobile device.

Benefits:

  • Hassle Free: Easy to operate and highly reliable system that makes it user friendly and maintenance free.

  • Lowers Labour Cost: Automates valve operations in irrigation which saves on the cost spent on labour.

  • Reduces Effort: Human effort in reaching valves spread over acres and determining the correct valve position is eliminated.

  • Effective Utilization of Water by Crop: The crop receives optimal quantity of water at the required intervals of time. Water is not wasted, enabling the crop to grow at maximum productivity.

  • Saves Electricity: Pump operates only during timings as required by the crop and hence wasteful usage of electricity is avoided.

How to Buy:

  1. Book an appointment using the button below. Provide the required details.

  2. Field Engineer will visit the field to study implementation.

  3. An estimation will be provided by us based on Field Engineer's report.

  4. After payment, installation and commissioning will be completed on the field by the field engineer. Engineer will also train the farmer on how to use the system.

Support:

  • Replacement of Non-Working Items: Items that are not working in the system will be identified and replaced. All spare items required for the system will be available.

  • Maintenance on Demand: Engineers are at your service to maintain the system on your request.

  • Field Support on Operations: Engineers will help operate the system on field to enable the farmer to do the same.
