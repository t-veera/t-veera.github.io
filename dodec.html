Creative Game Play

Player Transformation

Our Users:



Dodec Lite is a football analysis software tool for coaches, managers and players. Making game play and analyzing easier, Dodec supports player selection, match analysis and strategy evaluation in real-time.

Our Features:

Visualization

Compare game attributes of the teams playing the current match through transformation of data into graphical forms to enhance the meaning in context of the game.

Timeline Mode

Follow the critical data related to events of the match in real-time. Never miss a goal scored, goal saved, fouls, penalties and much more.

Communication & Coordination Indicator

Analyse team effort using Communication and Coordination Indicator to identify players with the best communication and coordination with the rest of the team players.

Formation Analysis

Mock and analyse the formations of both teams playing in the current match. Also, assists in creating new formations for strategy changes.

Possession Analysis

Analyse the ball possession of each team in the various parts of the field. Find strengths and weaknesses of teams based on the ability to keep the ball in possession.

Energy of the Game

Find the amount of energy spent by each team during a specific time interval. Follow the rise or fall in energy of the team in a given match.

Player Selection

Identify and analyse the right player to play against an opposition in the optimum position. Create the best possible combination of players for every match.

Strategy Analysis

Create, modify, store and implement game strategies. Evaluate strategies and identify the best strategy for a given opposition team and develop a game plan for the entire match.
