Agritex 2017 was the 5th international agricultural, diary and food processing trade fair. It was organized by Kenes Exhibition and sponsored by ICRISAT with cooperation from Telangana government.The event took place from 7th-9th September 2017 at Hitex exhibition grounds, Hyderabad and the event was attended by 50,000 farmers, dealers, potential buyers and educationists from all over the country. It exhibited a wide range of agricultural machinery and recent innovations by start-up companies. The purpose of the event was to promote business opportunities for farmers and help them connect with potential buyers and dealers.

We participated in the exhibition and showcased our latest innovative technology in the field of irrigation. Algoart's product, Hasmart Irrigation System, is an autonomous irrigation system which is capable of taking intelligent decisions on its own and implementing the process of irrigation in a timely and efficient manner. The merits of this system include:

  • Increased production.

  • Increased income for farmers.

  • Conservation of water.

  • Conservation of electricity.

  • Reduction in field visits.

  • Reduction in labour costs.

We had a satisfactory presentation and the response of the partakers was good. Over 200 farmers showed interest in our product. Most of the interested buyers were farmers inhabited in various cities. Our product gave them excellent control over their large agricultural fields (over 20 acres) and eased their field management. Our irrigation system provides hassle-free monitoring of irrigation and makes managing fields effortless and efficient, further promoting agricultural production and hassle-free entrepreneurship.
